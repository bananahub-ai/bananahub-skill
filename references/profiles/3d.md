## Behavioral Notes

- 3D renders legitimately use both photographic language (lens, DoF) AND illustration language (stylized, cel-shaded) — do not restrict vocabulary
- Isometric is NOT miniature/tilt-shift — do not add "miniature" or "tilt-shift" unless user requests it
- For architecture, always include human-scale figures or familiar objects to establish scale
- Clay renders are intentionally texture-free — do not add material descriptions for clay style
- When unsure between photorealistic and stylized, default to stylized 3D (more forgiving, better results for most subjects)
